Scrambled Eggs

I think we all know how to make scrambled eggs

But can you make them the Wyatt way?

Well Actually it's just Gordon Ramsay From "Binging With Babish"

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • pot, no a literally cooking pot
  • silocone spatula

How to make nice and fluffy eggs

  1. crack an even number of eggs that you want into a bowl
  2. mix them eggs to combine yolk/whites
  3. Add half the amount of butter by tbsp per egg. Eg: 8 eggs = 4 tbsp of butter
  4. Stir on low-medium heat until eggs are nice and fluffy
